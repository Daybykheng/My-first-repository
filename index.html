Ingredients You'll Need to Create This Khmer Dish

To make 2-3 servings, you will need:

  • 300g pork (shoulder or loin)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp coconut milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar or brown sugar
  • Steamed rice (preferred jasmine)
  • Pickled vegetables (optional)

Image from: Pixabay

Here's How to Set the Foundaion for Great Taste

Follow these steps to marinate the pork:

  • Mix soy sauce, coconut milk, sugar, and garlic in a bowl.
  • Add the pork slices and mix well.
  • Let it marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight.

Image from: Unsplash

Cooking the Pork and Rice

Time to cook the pork and rice:

  • Grill the pork slices for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  • Cook your rice while the pork is grilling.

Image from: Pixabay

It's Ready and How It's Enjoyed in Cambodia

Place the grilled pork over rice. Add pickles and soup on the side if you like. You can also top it with green onions or a fried egg.
